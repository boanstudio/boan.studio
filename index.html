BOAN consists of an artist/designer duo that bends technology to the will of performers. Their focus is on displays, projections, sets and wearables that respond to sound, movement, choreography and biometrics. Creating new avenues for conversation between performer and audience while opening the space for serendipity and improvisation.

BOAN is Boris Kourtoukov and Andreea Tecusan. Direct all inquiries to@boan.studio.
